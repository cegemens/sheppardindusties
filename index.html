Gunsmith & Firearms Specialist

C. Egemen Sheppard — Founder & Gunsmith.

With nearly a decade of experience as a certified NRA firearms instructor, I’ve dedicated my career to empowering responsible firearm ownership through education, precision, and craftsmanship. My journey into gunsmithing began with a five-year apprenticeship under a master gunsmith, where I honed my skills in firearm diagnostics, repair, and customization. I hold Glock LEO Armorer certification and specialize in servicing law enforcement needs with meticulous attention to detail. Beyond the bench, I’ve brokered firearm sales for private clients and collectors — offering tailored sourcing for rare and specific guns, and guiding clients through every step of the acquisition process. Whether you're seeking expert maintenance, custom builds, or consultation on firearm selection, I bring a blend of technical mastery and client-first professionalism to every project.

- Serving military, law enforcement, competitive and casual shooters, and collectors who demand perfection.
